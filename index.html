Accredited Courses

Coffee is a way of making time that rightfully belongs to your older, wiser self.

Introduction to coffe

Introduction to Coffee is an introductory course. The coffee skills programme consists of this introductory course and five modules: barista, brewing, green coffee, roasting and sensory skills.

Barista skills foundation

The Barista Skills Foundation is part of the SCA Coffee Skills Program and is the first course in the module dedicated to espresso brewing.

Sensory skills foundation

The Sensory Skills Foundation is part of the SCA Coffee Skills Program and is the first course in the module dedicated to sensory evaluation of coffee.
