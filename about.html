About Us

Our story

Our story begins in 2012, when The Coffe Shop was founded, and in a short time we managed to develop a successful business in the field of coffee and other complementary products. Since October 2012, The Coffe Shop is the official partner and importer of Tchibo Coffee Service GmbH in Romania.
